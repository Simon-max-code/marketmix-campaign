The People Behind MarketMix

Meet the Founders

Every great platform begins with people who believe things can be done differently. MarketMix was founded by two individuals with a shared vision: to build a marketplace where businesses of every size have the opportunity to grow, connect with customers, and compete on the strength of their products, not the size of their marketing budget.

Although we come from different backgrounds and bring different strengths to the table, we're united by one purpose: making commerce more accessible, more inclusive, and more rewarding for everyone. Thank you for being part of the journey, we invite you to meet the people behind MarketMix.

Co-founder & CEO
Oguebuka Emmanuel
Co-founder & CEO

Hello, I'm Simon, Co-founder & CEO of MarketMix.

I didn't create MarketMix because I wanted to build just another marketplace. I created it because I saw a problem that thousands of small business owners face every day.

Too many talented entrepreneurs have great products but struggle to reach customers. They're told they need expensive websites, paid advertisements, huge social media followings, or large amounts of capital before they can succeed online. Meanwhile, larger businesses continue to dominate the market simply because they have more resources.

I believe business growth shouldn't be reserved for those with the biggest budgets.

That's why I built MarketMix, a platform designed to give everyday entrepreneurs, creators, and business owners a fair opportunity to showcase their products, connect with customers, and grow their brands without unnecessary barriers.

My vision is simple: create a marketplace where opportunity is accessible, businesses can thrive, and buyers can discover great products from people they may never have found otherwise.

MarketMix is more than a platform to me. It's a mission to make digital commerce more inclusive, more accessible, and more rewarding for everyone involved.

Thank you for being part of the journey.

Co-founder & CTO
Emele Emmanuel
Co-Founder & CTO

Hi, I'm Emele Emmanuel, the Co-founder and CTO of MarketMix.

MarketMix wasn't born in a boardroom or funded by deep pockets, it was born from a real frustration I witnessed every single day. Brilliant entrepreneurs. Incredible products. And yet, almost no one knew they existed.

Across every street, every community, every city, there are gifted business owners who pour their hearts into what they do, only to be told they need expensive websites, paid ads, massive social media followings, or large capital before anyone will take them seriously. That never sat right with me. Talent and hard work should speak louder than budget size.

So I built MarketMix, not just as a marketplace, but as a movement. A movement that says your product deserves to be seen. A movement that tears down the walls that have kept everyday entrepreneurs on the outside of digital commerce for far too long. A movement that puts powerful tools, real visibility, and genuine opportunity in the hands of those who need it most.

My vision is bold but clear: a world where any entrepreneur, regardless of their resources, can showcase what they've built, connect with the right customers, and grow a brand they're proud of. Where buyers discover amazing products from people they would never have found otherwise. Where the playing field is finally, truly level.

MarketMix is more than a platform to me. It's a promise that ambition and dedication will always be enough to get you started. Thank you for believing in that promise with me. This journey is ours.
